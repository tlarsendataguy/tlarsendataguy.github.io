## Flight Path Images

I fly to get away.

To be alone.

To re-energize.

Most of the time I fly at night. I used to spend my nights practicing maneuvers or hopping to nearby airports. One night, I attempted to draw a picture with my flight path. I had seen it done before and thought it would be fun. And while the first attempt was a failure, I managed to scratch out a picture on my second.

​This site is a log of the pictures I have made. The most recent flights are on top. I hope you enjoy.
